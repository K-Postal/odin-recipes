Eggplant Pasta

This recipe for Eggplant Pasta has been in my rotation for a while now. It is a great recipe for those nights where you don't know what you want to make. It is a quick dish that never dissapoints.

It is traditionally fried in oil but you do have a choice of baking it. Personally I prefer the fried version but either way you won't be dissapointed.

Ingredients

    Pasta Tomato Passata White Wine Garlic and Onion Italian Herb Mix or Oregano Chilli flakes Basil
